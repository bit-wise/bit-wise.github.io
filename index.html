Iterative Digital Art by nick barcomb

living.color

Living Color explores the ideas of evolution and homeostasis. With each pass, each intersection's color is sampled and applied as the color of a line drawn in one of eight directions from that intersection. There is also a 1/1000 chance of a random color being applied, which simulated a mutation. This results in the formation of groups and struggles for dominance on those group's boundaries.

line.weaver

Line Weaver explores the idea of complexity from simplicity. With each pass, a random point is choosen on one of the four edges of the screen and a line is drawn to the other side. The only rule is that, if the line, as it is drawn, encounters another line, it toggles it's broken state. Imagine drawning a line and if you encounter another line, you lift your pen, then if encountering another you drawn again. This results in a highly complex pattern only possible by iteration.
